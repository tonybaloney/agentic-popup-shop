Retailers like Zava Stores deserve partners who share that vision.

## **4. Quality Assurance & Performance Standards**

Synthetic Solutions operates under ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certifications. Our quality framework includes:

- **Pre-production sampling:** All orders begin with approved pre-production samples; zero tolerance for deviations
- **In-line inspection:** Real-time quality monitoring during manufacturing; 100% batch testing for durability and fit
- **Third-party audits:** Independent lab testing for tensile strength, colorfastness, and chemical compliance (REACH, CPSIA)
- **Performance guarantees:** 
  - Defect rate <0.5% across all shipments
  - On-time delivery: ≥98% of orders meet agreed lead times
  - Full replacement or credit for any non-conforming materials
